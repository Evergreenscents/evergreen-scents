About Evergreen Scents

Evergreen Scents is a small, family-run business based in the beautiful Forest of Dean, England. What began as a simple passion for creating comforting fragrances has grown into a heartfelt business dedicated to handcrafting candles and wax melts that fill homes with warmth, joy, and nature’s charm.

Each of our candles and wax melts is handmade with love, using only high-quality, vegan-friendly ingredients. We offer a wide range of fragrances and styles — from rustic candles to elegantly shaped creations — all poured and finished by hand. Whether you prefer subtle, relaxing scents or bold, uplifting aromas, we craft each piece to perfection so you can experience pure bliss in every burn.

The images shown across our site are for illustration purposes — colours and sizes may vary slightly, as each product is lovingly handmade and unique. However, we promise that every item you receive will be just as beautiful, fragrant, and full of character as the photos you see.

Sustainability is at the heart of what we do. We use recyclable materials wherever possible, and we’re proud to say that all of our products are 100% vegan-friendly. We believe small steps make a big difference, and we’re always working to create products that are kind to both you and the planet.

Occasionally, we also host craft hours for children — fun, creative sessions where little ones can make their own candles to gift to loved ones on special occasions like Mother’s Day or Christmas. These experiences are full of laughter, learning, and creativity — leaving each participant with something truly special they can proudly call their own.

From our family to yours, thank you for supporting a small local business. We hope Evergreen Scents brings the same warmth and joy into your home that we feel every time we create.
