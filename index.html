Welcome to Evergreen Scents

Step into the warm and rustic world of Evergreen Scents, where every candle and wax melt is handmade in the heart of the Forest of Dean, England. Each product is crafted with love, passion, and care — from the first pour to the final packaging.

Our scents are rich, long-lasting, and designed to fill your home with comfort. Whether you choose our beautifully shaped candles or our wonderfully fragrant wax melts, you’ll discover why each creation is a little piece of nature’s magic.

Enjoy exploring our shop, gallery, and story — and experience the art of handmade fragrance that’s been lovingly perfected for your home.
